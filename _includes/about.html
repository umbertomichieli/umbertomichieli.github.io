About

I was born in Italy in 1994. I got my BS and MS respectively in Information Engineering (2016) and in Telecommunication Engineering (2018) from the University of Padova.
Now I am a Ph.D. student at the Department of Information Engineering of the same University under the supervision of Prof. Pietro Zanuttigh.

My research interests include applications of Game Theory, Semantic Segmentation and Deep Learning for images and videos, link-prediction in Complex Networks.
A particular attention is given to the adoption of Generative Adversarial Networks for semi-supervised Semantic Segmentation in order to dramatically reduce the number of training samples needed.

Check the publications section to know more about these topics and the Multimedia Technology and Telecommunication Lab at University of Padova.
