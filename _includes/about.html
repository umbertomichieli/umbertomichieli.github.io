About

I am a Computer Vision and Deep Learning research scientist. My goal is to contribute to the continual adaptation of AI to human needs and its adoption into everyday life.

I was born in Italy in 1994. I got my B.Sc and M.Sc. respectively in Information Engineering (2016) and in Telecommunication Engineering (2018) from the University of Padova.
Currently, I am a final-year Ph.D. candidate at the Department of Information Engineering of the same University under the supervision of Pietro Zanuttigh in the LTTM lab.

My main research interests are:
- Continual Learning: train deep learning models to incrementally learn new concepts over time.
- Unsupervised Domain Adaptation: adapt deep neural networks trained on large datasets, where the generation of the ground-truth is inexpensive, to other data without labels.
- Federated Learning: collaboratively train deep learning models without sharing private user data.
- Coarse-to-Fine Curriculum Learning: refine a coarser training with a finer set of labels.
