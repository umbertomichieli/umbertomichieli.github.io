About

I am a Computer Vision and Deep Learning research scientist. My goal is to contribute to the continual adaptation of AI to human needs and its adoption into everyday life.

I was born in Italy in 1994. I got my B.Sc and M.Sc. respectively in Information Engineering (2016) and in Telecommunication Engineering (2018) from the University of Padova.
Currently, I am a Ph.D. student at the Department of Information Engineering of the same University under the supervision of Pietro Zanuttigh.

My main research interests are:
- Continual Learning: deep learning models should incrementally learn new concepts over time.
- Unsupervised Domain Adaptation: deep neural networks trained on large datasets, where the generation of the ground-truth is inexpensive, should be adapted to other data without labels.
- Federated Learning: to collaboratively train deep learning models without sharing private user data.

Check out the publications section to know more about these topics and the Multimedia Technology and Telecommunication Lab at University of Padova.
