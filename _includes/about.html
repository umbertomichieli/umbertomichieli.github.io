About

I am a Computer Vision and Deep Learning enthusiast and my goal is to encourage the deployment of AI into everyday life.

I was born in Italy in 1994. I got my B.Sc and M.Sc. respectively in Information Engineering (2016) and in Telecommunication Engineering (2018) from the University of Padova.
Currently, I am a Ph.D. student at the Department of Information Engineering of the same University under the supervision of Pietro Zanuttigh.

My main research interests are:
- Continual Learning for semantic segmentation: deep learning models should incrementally learn to segment and to label new classes over time.
- Unsupervised Domain Adaptation for semantic segmentation: we would like to train deep neural networks on large synthetic datasets, where the generation of the ground-truth is inexpensive, and then adapt them to real dataset without labels.

Check out the publications section to know more about these topics and the Multimedia Technology and Telecommunication Lab at University of Padova.
